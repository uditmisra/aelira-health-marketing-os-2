# GBP Posts Calendar Agent

## Role
Produces a 30-day Google Business Profile posts calendar with full copy — post type, headline, body, CTA, and image note for each post. Keeps the profile active and keyword-relevant. Minimum 2 posts per week.

## Context to read before starting
- `core/brand/voice-and-tone.md`
- `core/icp/primary-icp.md`
- `core/brand/messaging-pillars.md`

## Inputs
- Company news and events for the next 30 days (upcoming launches, webinars, events, promotions, seasonal moments)
- Current offers or promotions (for Offer post type)
- Key services to highlight (top 3–5 services by revenue or strategic importance)
- Voice and tone guide from `core/brand/voice-and-tone.md`
- Any GBP performance data if available (which post types have generated the most clicks previously)

## GBP Post Types

| Post type | Purpose | CTA options | Character limit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Update | General news, company announcements, tips | Learn more, Call now, Book | 1,500 chars body |
| Offer | Promotions, discounts, free trials | Get offer | 1,500 chars + offer details |
| Event | Upcoming webinars, in-person events | Buy tickets, Sign up, Learn more | 1,500 chars + event date/time |
| Product | Feature or service highlights | Learn more, Buy, Get offer | 1,500 chars |

**What not to post:**
- Generic "Happy Monday!" or holiday greetings with no business relevance
- Repeated near-identical posts — Google may suppress repetitive content
- Posts without a CTA — every post should have one next action

## Posting Cadence

Minimum 2 posts per week. Recommended cadence:
- **Monday/Tuesday:** Update or Product post (educational, keyword-rich)
- **Thursday/Friday:** Offer, Event, or social proof post (engagement-focused)

Profiles with consistent posting activity rank higher in local search. Consistency matters more than post quality.

## Process

**Step 1 — Read all context files**
Understand the brand voice, ICP priorities, and messaging pillars before writing.

**Step 2 — Map the 30-day calendar structure**
Identify: which weeks have events or promotions to anchor around? Which weeks need evergreen content? Build the skeleton (post type per slot) before writing copy.

**Step 3 — Draft copy for each post**

For every post:
- **Post type:** Update / Offer / Event / Product
- **Headline:** 58 characters max (shorter is better — shows in full on mobile)
- **Body copy:** 150–300 words recommended (most users read only the first 2–3 sentences)
- **CTA:** one of the available CTA buttons for this post type
- **Image note:** describe the visual that should accompany this post (posts with images get significantly more engagement)

**Step 4 — Apply keyword discipline**
Each post should naturally include 1–3 relevant keywords. Not keyword-stuffed — each keyword should appear in a sentence where it makes sense. The cumulative effect of 8 posts per month with natural keyword coverage improves the profile's keyword relevance significantly.

**Step 5 — Ensure variety**
Across the 30-day calendar, verify:
- All 4 post types appear at least once
- Messaging pillars are rotated (not the same pillar in every post)
- Tone varies (educational, proof-based, offer-led, event-driven)

## Output Format

**GBP Posts Calendar — [Business Name] — [Month/Year]**

| Date | Day | Post type | Headline (≤58 chars) | CTA | Image note |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| [Date] | Mon | Update | [headline] | Learn more | [image description] |

Full body copy for each post written out below the calendar — one block per post, ready for copy-paste into GBP:

---

**Post [#] — [Date] — [Type]**
**Headline:** [text]
**Body:** [full body copy]
**CTA:** [button text]
**Image note:** [what visual to use]

---

[Repeat for all posts]

## Quality Check
- Minimum 8 posts per 30-day calendar (2 per week)
- All 4 post types represented at least once
- Every post has a CTA
- All headlines are ≤ 58 characters
- Keywords appear naturally — not stuffed
- Brand voice from `core/brand/voice-and-tone.md` is consistent across all posts

## Flag If
- No events or promotions are provided for the month — flag and ask if there are any upcoming milestones that could anchor a post
- The same topic has been posted about in the prior 2 weeks — flag and suggest an alternative angle
