## Positioning Statement

**For** urban Delhi residents with lung health concerns — whether living with a diagnosed condition, recovering from COVID, or simply worried about years of breathing polluted air —
**Aelira Lung Care** is the **dedicated lung centre** that
**gives you a clear, comprehensive picture of your lung health** — with advanced tests unavailable at general diagnostic labs, results before you leave, and a calm environment that hospital OPDs can’t offer.

**Unlike** hospital pulmonology departments (which fit you around their schedule and give you a basic spirometry report in three days) and general diagnostic chains (which give you 6 metrics and a queue number), **Aelira does one thing, deeply**: lung diagnostics and rehabilitation, with the depth and attention your lungs deserve.

---

## Primary Claim

**“Delhi’s dedicated lung centre. Advanced tests. Your results before you leave.”**

This is the claim no hospital department or diagnostic chain can credibly make simultaneously. It combines:
- Specialization (dedicated)
- Advanced testing (FOT + FeNO + DLCO, not just spirometry)
- Experience (same-day results, calm environment)

---

## Key Differentiator

**Advanced testing + immediate comprehensive results, from a centre that does nothing but lungs.**

The FOT (Lung Oscillometry) test is the clinical anchor: it detects small airway disease that standard spirometry misses, requires no forced breathing effort (accessible for elderly and children), and is not available at most Delhi diagnostic centers or hospital OPDs. Paired with FeNO (airway inflammation), DLCO (gas transfer), and comprehensive PFT, Aelira’s testing panel is categorically more complete than any competitor’s in the South Delhi area.

The immediate, same-day, 50+ metric report is the experience anchor.

## Messaging Pillars (3)

### Pillar 1: The Most Complete Lung Picture in Delhi
*What we say: Aelira is the only centre in South Delhi where you can get PFT + FeNO + Lung Oscillometry (FOT) + DLCO in one visit, with results the same day.*

Why it works: Specific, verifiable, directly competitive with the diagnostic chain alternative (6 metrics vs. 50+). Speaks to the patient who has had a basic spirometry elsewhere and wasn’t satisfied.

Messages to build:
- “50+ metrics. Results before you leave.”
- “Standard spirometry misses small airway disease. Lung Oscillometry catches it.”
- “Four tests in one visit: PFT, FeNO, FOT, DLCO.”

### Pillar 2: Built for Delhi’s Lungs
*What we say: Aelira was built for a city where the air is making people sick — and where most people don’t know the damage until symptoms are already serious.*

Why it works: The Delhi air quality crisis is a massive, recurring demand driver. Pollution-related lung anxiety is one of the most common reasons proactive patients seek testing. No competitor is running this narrative. Aelira can own it.

Messages to build:
- “Delhi’s air quality is a lung health emergency. Most people don’t find out how it’s affected them until it’s hard to undo.”
- “Your GP checks your heart and blood pressure. Who’s checking your lungs?”
- “For people who live in a city with AQI 300, a lung assessment is not a luxury.”

### Pillar 3: Rehabilitation That Gets You Moving Again
*What we say: Aelira’s pulmonary rehab program is the one recommended by the American Thoracic Society, WHO, and British Thoracic Society — and it’s available in South Delhi, from a team that does nothing else.*

Why it works: Bridges the gap between diagnosis and the rehab patient’s goal (function, not just lung numbers). The global guideline endorsement builds credibility that a standalone centre needs to establish vs. hospital brand trust.

Messages to build:
- “Breathlessness is not something to adapt to. It’s something to treat.”
- “Clinically proven to reduce breathlessness by 25–50% in published studies.”
- “The only dedicated pulmonary rehabilitation centre in South Delhi.”

## Avoid

- “Holistic care” — too generic, sounds like wellness not medicine
- “World-class” / “best-in-class” — unearned without specific proof
- “Comprehensive” as a standalone adjective (use it WITH the specific metric list)
- “Patient-centered care” — every clinic claims this; meaningless
- “State-of-the-art” — empty; describe the actual technology (oscillometry, FeNO analyzer)
- Category-level “lung health awareness” messaging without a specific claim or action

---

## What We’re NOT Saying (and Why)

- **We are not saying we’re cheaper.** Aelira is a premium specialist experience. Price is not the positioning.
- **We are not claiming to treat every lung condition.** We diagnose and rehabilitate. Bronchoscopy, HRCT, interventional procedures — that’s a hospital’s job. We refer when needed.
- **We are not making outcome guarantees.** We cite published research (“studies show 25–50% reduction in breathlessness”) — we don’t promise individual outcomes.
