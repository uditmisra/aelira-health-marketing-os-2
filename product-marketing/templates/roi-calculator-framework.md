## How to use this framework

Built in two modes:

1. **Internal prep:** Complete Sections 1-4 before the business case meeting. Inputs in Section 1 come from discovery — if you don't have them, go back to discovery before building the case.
2. **Prospect-facing output:** Sections 3-5 are designed to be shared. The assumptions appendix (Section 6) is critical — transparency about assumptions builds credibility with CFOs and skeptical buyers.

A business case that hides its assumptions loses credibility the moment someone stress-tests a number. Show the math.

## Section 7: What This Model Does Not Include

_Intellectual honesty. Value categories that are real but not quantified._

- [ e.g., "Employee satisfaction and retention: reduced manual work often correlates with reduced turnover, but we did not quantify this." ]
