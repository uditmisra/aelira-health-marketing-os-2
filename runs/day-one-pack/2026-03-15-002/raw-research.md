## 2. Product / Service Intelligence

### Services or products offered
- **Pulmonary Function Tests (PFT) / Spirometry** — measures airflow and lung volumes
- **DLCO Test (Gas Transfer / Diffusing Capacity)** — measures how well gas passes from lungs to bloodstream
- **FeNO Test (Fractional Exhaled Nitric Oxide)** — non-invasive airway inflammation marker; helps distinguish asthma from other conditions
- **FOT Test / Lung Oscillometry (Impulse Oscillometry)** — measures airway resistance including small airways; can be done with tidal breathing (easier for children and elderly)
- **Pulmonary Rehabilitation** — comprehensive, structured, evidence-based program covering: breathing retraining, supervised exercise, breathlessness management, muscle conditioning, airway clearance, medication/inhaler optimization, oxygen therapy guidance, disease education, nutritional counselling

### What makes them different (their own claim)
Site repeats: “exclusive centre” (standalone specialty vs. hospital department), “evidence-based” programs, “calm healing environment,” immediate report delivery, comprehensive 50+ metric reports, suitability for “children, adults, and older adults.”

The FOT / Lung Oscillometry test is notably advanced — most Delhi PFT labs offer only spirometry. Aelira offering FOT is a genuine clinical differentiator.

Endorsed by: American Thoracic Society, British Thoracic Society, European Respiratory Society, WHO (shown on rehab page as logos)

### Technology or methodology signals
- FOT (Oscillometry): rare in Delhi outpatient/standalone setting
- FeNO: non-invasive airway inflammation test; helps catch asthma under-diagnosis
- DLCO: differentiates from basic spirometry-only labs
- Rehab: globally recognized evidence-based model (ATS/BTS/ERS/WHO endorsed)
- Report detail: “over 50 metrics” vs. typical spirometry reports

### Pricing signals
- MISSING from website
- Competitive context: Basic PFT at diagnostic chains: INR 1,500–2,500. Specialist clinic with FOT/FeNO/DLCO likely premium: estimated INR 2,500–5,000+ per session.
- Rehab programs nationally: typically INR 15,000–50,000+ for a 6–12 week course.
