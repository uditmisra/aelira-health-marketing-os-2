## White Space

1. **“The standalone specialist lung centre for South Delhi”** — No competitor is a dedicated lung-only outpatient centre specifically serving South Delhi/Green Park. Max and Yashoda are hospitals. NITRD is government. Diagnostic chains are generalist. Aelira is categorically alone in this position.

2. **“Comprehensive lung testing beyond spirometry”** — No competitor foregrounds FOT/Oscillometry + FeNO + DLCO as a combined offering. Most competitors offer spirometry only. Aelira’s advanced testing menu is both a clinical differentiator and a positioning opportunity.

3. **“For the polluted city that knows it’s polluted”** — No competitor is running a Delhi-specific pollution + lung health narrative. The macro context (AQI 350+, "25 cigarettes a day" news cycles) creates a massive demand tailwind that Aelira can own narratively.

---

## Recommended Posture

**Challenger** — Aelira is new, has limited brand recognition, and competes against established hospital names. However, Aelira holds a genuinely differentiated position that none of the incumbents can easily replicate (standalone specialist + advanced testing + experience focus). The strategy: don’t out-spend Max on brand awareness. Out-position them by owning the category frame they’re too large and generalist to credibly claim.

Specifically: let Max be the “hospital with a pulmonology department.” Aelira should be “the dedicated lung centre” — a fundamentally different category of care.
