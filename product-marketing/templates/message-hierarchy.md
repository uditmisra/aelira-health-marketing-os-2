## How to use this document

**This is the source of truth for all marketing claims.**

Every piece of copy — ads, email, website, sales deck, launch blog — should be traceable to something in this hierarchy. If you are writing copy and cannot connect it to a pillar and a proof point, you are either making a claim we cannot support, or you have found a gap in the hierarchy that needs to be added.

The messaging brief (at the bottom) is the version for daily use. The full template is the archival source.
