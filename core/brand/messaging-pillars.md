# Messaging Pillars — SpotDraft

> Produced by: initial onboarding (to be refined by message-hierarchy-builder after positioning sprint)
> Last updated: 2026-03-12
> Flag for refresh: if win rate drops below 25% or competitive displacement by Ironclad exceeds 40% in a quarter

## Primary claim

SpotDraft is the contract management platform built for in-house legal teams who need to move the business fast — not spend more time managing software.

**Alt for ads (problem-led):** Your contracts shouldn't live in someone's inbox. SpotDraft gives legal and business teams one place to create, review, sign, and track every contract.

**Alt for ads (outcome-led):** Faster contracts. Fewer risks. Less back-and-forth. SpotDraft.

---

## Pillar 1: Speed Without Sacrifice

**Message:** SpotDraft cuts contract turnaround from days to hours — without removing legal oversight.

**Proof points:**
- Customers report reducing average contract review time by 50–70%
- AI-assisted review surfaces non-standard clauses in minutes, not hours of manual reading
- Business teams self-serve on standard agreements (NDAs, MSAs) using pre-approved templates — no legal queue
- Razorpay: reduced contract turnaround from 5 days to 6 hours [confirm with customer]

**Objection this addresses:** "We need legal to review everything — we can't just automate contracts."

**How to use in ads:**
- Problem frame: "How many deals are waiting on a contract right now?"
- Outcome frame: "Go from contract request to signed in the same day."
- Social proof frame: "[Customer] cut contract review time by [X]% with SpotDraft."

**In-ads framing note:** Lead with the business pain (deals stuck, revenue delayed) not the legal team's workflow pain. The economic buyer (COO, CRO) is as important as the GC here.

---

## Pillar 2: Built for How Legal Teams Actually Work

**Message:** SpotDraft is the only CLM that legal teams actually adopt — because it fits their workflow, not the other way around.

**Proof points:**
- Setup in days, not months — no 6-month implementation project
- Native integrations with the tools legal already uses: Google Docs, Slack, Salesforce, HubSpot
- No dedicated IT or legal ops team required to manage it
- Template builder designed for non-technical users — GCs build their own playbooks
- Average time-to-first-contract: [X days — pull from customer data]

**Objection this addresses:** "We tried a CLM before and no one used it after 3 months."

**How to use in ads:**
- Pain frame: "The last CLM your team bought is probably collecting dust. Here's why SpotDraft is different."
- Ease frame: "Live in days, not months. No IT project. No consultant."
- Adoption frame: "Legal teams at [Company] went from zero to fully live in [X] days."

---

## Pillar 3: Visibility Across Every Contract

**Message:** SpotDraft gives legal and leadership complete visibility into every contract — active, pending, expiring, and at risk.

**Proof points:**
- Centralized contract repository with full-text search across every agreement
- Automated renewal and obligation tracking — no more missed deadlines
- Real-time status visibility for business teams (no more "is the contract signed yet?" Slack messages)
- Audit trail for every change, comment, and approval
- Executive dashboard: contract volume, average turnaround, bottleneck identification

**Objection this addresses:** "We can manage contracts fine with Google Drive / Dropbox."

**How to use in ads:**
- Risk frame: "Your next contract renewal is in 30 days. Do you know which one?"
- Control frame: "See every contract your company has ever signed. From one screen."
- Ops frame: "Finance asked you for a list of active vendor contracts. How long does that take right now?"

## What we are NOT saying

| Message we're not leading with | Why |
|---|---|
| "AI-powered CLM" | AI is table stakes now; saying it first sounds like a feature pitch |
| "The only CLM you'll ever need" | Sounds like a vendor, not a peer — plus it's unprovable |
| "Enterprise-grade security and compliance" | Not a differentiator at our ICP size; surfaces concern, not desire |
| "We integrate with 200+ tools" | Breadth doesn't win deals; specific integrations do |
| "SpotDraft was founded to change how legal works" | Founder story is not a buyer's problem; move it to About page |
