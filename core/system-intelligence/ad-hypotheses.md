## Active Hypotheses

### H-001
**Question:** Does problem-led framing outperform benefit-led (emotional) framing for inquiry generation at awareness/consideration stage?

**Background:** First ad batch (2026-03-15-003) includes parallel problem-led ("Getting breathless in Delhi?", "Delhi's air damaging your lungs?") and benefit-led emotional ads ("Finally know your lungs.", "Leave with answers today."). Gate 1 rejected technical benefit framing ("50+ metrics" as headline). Emotional rewrite passed. H-001 tests whether the emotional benefit frame competes with the problem frame on actual inquiry rate.

**Test design:**
- Problem-led: Ad Sets 1–2 (Meta), Campaign 1 (Google) — tagged [H001-P]
- Benefit-led emotional: Ad Sets 3–4 (Meta), Campaign 2 (Google) — tagged [H001-B]
- Control variables: same targeting, budget allocation, image, CTA type

**What confirms it:** Problem-led CTR + inquiry rate >20% higher than benefit-led at ≥95% confidence after 2+ weeks / ≥100 clicks per variant
**What refutes it:** No significant difference, or benefit-led outperforms
**Run IDs:** 2026-03-15-003
**Status:** Active — awaiting performance data
