## How to read this document

The five components must be read in order — each one constrains and informs the next. The chain is: who we compete with → what we do differently → why that matters → who cares most → how to set context so they get it immediately.

If any component feels wrong, identify which one and correct it. Everything downstream (messaging, ad copy, battlecards, launch narratives) derives from this canvas. A wrong component here produces consistently wrong output everywhere else.
