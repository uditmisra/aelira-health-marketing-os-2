## Key creative decisions logged

1. **Benefit-led headlines must lead emotionally, not technically.** "Finally know your lungs" outperformed "50+ metrics, same-day report" at Gate 1 review. Technical specs belong in copy layer, not headline.
2. **Delhi air / "25 cigarettes a day" is a high-resonance hook.** Used correctly (attributed to external source, not Aelira claim).
3. **Patient verbatims are the strongest social proof available.** Ankit (50+ metrics) and Reena (immediate report) are anchor quotes for this campaign.
4. **Comparison-led (vs. hospital OPD) is viable.** "Not a hospital. Only lungs." passed QG at 5/4/4 — use for hospital-intenders audience.
