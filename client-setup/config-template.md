# Client Configuration Template — Guided Questionnaire

## How to use this template

Work through each question with a collaborator who knows the business well — ideally someone who owns marketing strategy and someone who owns sales. For fields you're unsure about, put your best current answer and mark it `[NEEDS VALIDATION]`. Avoid leaving fields blank. A rough answer is better than an empty one because rough answers can be improved; blank fields produce generic output.

When complete, copy your answers into the corresponding fields in `CLAUDE.md` under `## Client Configuration`. The system is ready to run once every field has an answer.
