# Competitor: General Diagnostic Chains
(Ganesh Diagnostic, CNC Path Lab, City X-Ray, Mansi Neuro Imaging, and similar)

> Last updated: 2026-03-15
> Generated by: competitive-landscape-builder (Day 1 Pack run 2026-03-15-002)

## Overview
- Representative URLs: ganeshdiagnostic.com, cncpathlab.com, cityxrayclinic.com
- Positioning: “Affordable PFT tests near me” | “up to 70% off” | “transparent pricing”
- Primary market: Price-sensitive patients, GP-directed testing, insurance-linked diagnostics
- Pricing model: INR 1,500–2,500 for basic PFT/spirometry. Heavy discounting advertised ("upto 70% off").
- Funding / stage: Independent diagnostic chains, mostly unlisted

## Their narrative
General diagnostic chains position on access, price, and coverage. The message: “Get your test done near you at the lowest price.” Pulmonary testing is one of 50+ tests on their menu alongside MRI, blood work, and ECG. There is no specialization claim.

## Strengths (why they win)
- Lowest price point in the market (INR 1,500–2,500 vs. Aelira’s estimated premium)
- Multiple locations across Delhi NCR (geographic convenience)
- Often covered by or linked to GP referral discount packages
- Fast, walk-in or same-day appointment availability

## Weaknesses (why they lose)
- “Bad experience, they don’t care about you and lady on the counter knows only one thing and that is shouting loud” — Sulekha review, Mansi Neuro Imaging / Green Park diagnostic center
- “Beware of their fraud — they charged for MRI and gave a screening, not a study” — Sulekha review (same area)
- Spirometry only — no FOT, no FeNO, no DLCO in most locations. Cannot serve patients needing advanced testing.
- Reports typically cover 6–10 basic metrics — not the comprehensive picture Aelira provides
- No clinical interpretation or specialist involvement; technician-operated
- No pulmonary rehabilitation capability at all

## How to beat them
1. **The 50-metric comparison.** “A diagnostic chain gives you 6 numbers. We give you 50+ with clinical interpretation. If all you need is a number, they’re fine. If you want to understand your lungs, come to us.”
2. **The experience comparison.** Aelira’s reviews describe “clean and calming” and “best experience end-to-end.” Diagnostic chain reviews describe shouting staff and possible fraud. This is a stark contrast that can be communicated without naming names.
3. **The test range.** FOT, FeNO, and DLCO are tests that diagnostic chains don’t offer. If a GP has referred the patient for any of these, Aelira is the only option in the area.

## Their customers
Price-sensitive patients, patients directed by GP to “get a PFT done” without a specific clinic recommended, patients managing multiple diagnostic tests in one visit.

## Recent moves (last 90 days)
- No specific moves. Category is stable. Heavy price competition continues (70% discount advertising on Justdial/Sulekha).
